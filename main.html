We are the 21st Century Vintage Furniture Manufacturer based in Johannesburg with a nationwide footprint. We specialise in a variety of pieces, from household goodies to industrial supplies. Our kids furniture department ranks amongst the best and our team of talented individuals will surely get the job done to the satisfaction of our clients.
